Moving to Prioritize Adolescent Mental Health

Recent events have illuminated the great need for mental consideration of youth. Amid stressful times, those legally considered to be adults are not alone in being weighed down by international occurences. Children feel the burden of strenous times, they deserve emotional relief to prevent being overwhelmed by tension.

The Mental State of Adolescents Pre-Covid

Prior to Covid, a pandemic which illuminated fatal flaws in societal living, mental health advocacy remained unprioritized. Those whose mental inabilities went noticed were few in number, especially among youth. Such problems emphasize the need for improvements to prioritize the mental health of adolescents.
